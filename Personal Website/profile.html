My Interests

These are the things that fuel my designs and inform the look and feel of my crochet work.

  • Yarn Science & Sourcing: I love learning about ply, twist, and drape, and seeking out ethically produced, small-batch dyed yarns.
  • Texture Mapping: I experiment with Tunisian stitches, cables, and post-stitch variations to create deep, tactile surfaces.
  • Art History & Colorwork: Studying historical textiles inspires my palettes and use of tapestry crochet.
  • Swatch Meditation: My favorite way to unwind is crocheting test swatches.

Challenges

I'm still learning and improving every day. Here are some hurdles all serious crocheters face:

  • The Frogging Fear: I often unravel sections that aren't perfect.
  • Pattern-Writing Precision: Writing clear and size-inclusive patterns is hard.
  • The Single-Crochet Slump: Repetitive stitch rows test my focus.
  • Stash Control: Managing yarn stash is a constant struggle!

Weaving the Future: Goals

  • Inspire Future Designers by mentoring beginners.
